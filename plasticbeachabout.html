About Us

Plastic Beach is a publicly funded non-profit located in Houston, Texas. Our mission is to drive local action for global ocean conservation. We advance our mission through local beach clean ups and fun activites for kids by making “clean artwork”. By allowing families an easy way for their children to get involved in recycling and learning the importance of keeping the ocean clean, we hope that we can create a cleaner future for our future and allow creativity throught different avenues.
